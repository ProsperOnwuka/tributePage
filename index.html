Saint Louis Marie de Montfort

Apostle of Mary

Here's a timeline of St. Louis Marie de Montfort's life:

  • 1673 - Born on January 31 in Montfort-sur-Meu, France.
  • 1685 - Montfort enters the Jesuit College of St Thomas Becket in Rennes at the age of 12.
  • 1693 - He continues his studies of philosophy and theology at St Thomas in Rennes.
  • 1695 - Montfort goes to Paris to study at the Seminary of Saint-Sulpice but faces financial difficulties and lives among the poor.
  • 1695-1697 - Montfort becomes ill and is hospitalized but survives his illness and treatment. He studies spirituality and Marian devotion while working as a librarian at the Seminary of Saint-Sulpice.
  • 1700 - Montfort is ordained as a priest in June and is assigned to Nantes.
  • 1700 - He joins the Third Order of the Dominicans and begins preaching the Rosary and forming Rosary Confraternities.
  • 1703-1710 - Montfort preaches missions and retreats in various places, gaining a reputation as a missioner.
  • 1712 - He founds the Company of Mary, a small company of priests dedicated to preaching missions and retreats under the protection of the Blessed Virgin.
  • 1715 - Montfort establishes a school in La Rochelle with the help of Marie Louise Trichet and other followers.
  • 1715 - The Daughters of Wisdom, founded by Montfort, receive approbation from the Bishop of La Rochelle.
